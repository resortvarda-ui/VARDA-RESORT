Our Story

A Legacy of Timeless Elegance

Nestled amidst verdant landscapes and whispering pines, Varda Resort was born from a singular vision — to create a sanctuary where luxury meets authenticity. We have evolved into a premier destination for those seeking exceptional hospitality in Buxar.

Every corner of Varda tells a story. From the elegant marble in our grand lobby to the thoughtfully curated ingredients in our kitchens, we believe that true luxury lies in the details. Our commitment to excellence extends beyond opulence — it's woven into every interaction, every experience, and every memory we help create.

Today, Varda Resort stands as a testament to the belief that hospitality is an art form. We invite you to become part of our continuing story, where every stay is a chapter worth remembering.
